Our Story

Discover Sarvatta

Founded in 2010, Sarvatta Cafe began as a small coffee shop with big dreams. Our founder, Chef Rajiv Kapoor, envisioned a space where quality food, exceptional coffee, and warm hospitality would create memorable experiences for every guest.

Today, we've grown into a beloved neighborhood destination known for our artisanal pizzas, specialty coffees, and inviting atmosphere. Every dish we serve is crafted with locally sourced ingredients and prepared with passion.
